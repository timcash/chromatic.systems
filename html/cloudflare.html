Connect to Cloudflare

Setup a load balancer

  
                
                    $ cloudflared tunnel route lb $tunnel_id_name $load_balancer_name $load_balancer_pool

You can now route traffic to your Tunnel, and on to your local server, using Cloudflare DNS. Visit the Cloudflare dashboard, select a website, and click on the DNS tab. Click +Add record and choose CNAME. In the Name field, add the name of the subdomain of your new site. In the Content field, paste the ID of your Tunnel created earlier and append cfargotunnel.com. 5157d321-5933-4b30-938b-d889ca87e11b.cfargotunnel.com
